## Proposal templates (not site content, but for the studio's pitch motion)

These live OUTSIDE the website but get referenced from the contact flow.

### Single-page Quick Look Proposal (IMG_3033)
1. Who we are & what we do / services
2. Objectives with partnership — JD/client needs / quick overview
3. Proposed strategy w/ value prop — "We customize to your business needs"
4. The Hook — acknowledge the role they're trying to fill but highlight the benefits of organized team. Value prop: NOT fractional, retained, costs less than salary, training, benefits w/ onboarding
5. Next steps — program that plugs w/ company needs & goals. "Our agency could step in immediately & execute your strategy vs. full time"

### Full Proposal (IMG_3034)
1. Who we are & how we work
2. Objectives & alignment w/ JD/needs
3. Proposed strategy & tiers (Tier 1 / Tier 2 / +1 / Add-On)
4. Value prop & overcome in-house objection — use a chart: in-house vs agency
5. Next steps & timeline — meet to define objectives/needs → team dives into brand → next steps
6. Investment
7. Closing & next — "Where Creative Meets Conversion"
