## The Palm Social Club (slides 3-6)

**Original framing:**
- Brand Concept • Hospitality Design • Creative Direction
- "The Palm Social Club was developed as a hospitality concept rooted in the creative culture of Port Aransas — blending live music, design, and social experience into a cohesive brand environment."
- "The concept reimagined the traditional coastal bar as a design-forward, community-driven venue inspired by vintage hotel lounges, tropical interiors, and historic social clubs."
- "I led the full brand vision, defining the identity, atmosphere, and experience strategy to create a destination that felt both elevated and deeply local."

**Role:** Led concept dev + brand identity · Directed interior experience, visual language, spatial design · Oversaw creative across brand, environment, programming · Managed collaboration across design, build, ops teams

**Brand experience & creative direction:** "Designed as an immersive brand experience — not just a venue. All aspects from interiors to programming developed to create a cohesive environment where music, design, and community intersect. Visual direction drew from vintage hospitality, coastal modernism, and local history."

**Outcomes:** Strong cultural presence in Port Aransas · Central gathering space for locals, visitors, musicians, creatives · Curated music program + elevated beverages · Repeat engagement

**Color palette:** Warm Black · Dark Green · Sage Green · Mustard · Coral · Magenta

## The Palm Republic (slides 10-17)

**Original framing:**
- Lifestyle Brand • Founder • Creative Director
- "The Palm Republic is a lifestyle brand inspired by the culture and community of Port Aransas, Texas."
- "I founded and led the brand from inception — developing identity, product strategy, and retail experience."
- "The brand operates as both a commercial business and a cultural platform — connecting people through place and shared identity."

**Brand origin:** "Created in the aftermath of Hurricane Harvey as a unifying symbol for the Port Aransas community. What began as a single flag and t-shirt fundraiser evolved into a larger brand platform rooted in resilience, pride, and identity."

**Brand identity & system:** "Developed a scalable brand system across retail, product, and digital platforms. Includes logo family, typography, and a coastal-inspired color palette applied across apparel, packaging, retail, and digital."

**Brand experience & retail:** "Led development of multiple retail environments translating brand identity into physical space. Integrated merchandising, storytelling, and spatial design to create consistent brand experiences."

**Creative collaborations:** GET OUTSIDE × PALM REPUBLIC · "Directed campaigns and content across product launches and marketing initiatives — from concept through execution."

**Community & cultural impact:** "The brand serves as a platform for events, partnerships, and cultural engagement. Creates ongoing connection through design, storytelling, brand loyalty and community."
